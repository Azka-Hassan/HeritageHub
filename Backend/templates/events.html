Kalash Choimus Festival held at Kalash

Chilam Joshi is also known as Kalash spring festival. Chilam Joshi festival is held in three Kalash valleys, Bumburet, Birir, and Rumbur where Kalash girls and boys dance to the tune of traditional drum beats. Chilam Joshi celebrations highlight their cultural richness, the plethora of colors, and the underlying message of peace.
